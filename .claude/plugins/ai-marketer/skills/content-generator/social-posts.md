### Thread Structure (7-10 tweets)

1. **Hook** - Stop the scroll (problem/promise)
2. **Agitate** - Make them feel the pain
3. **Backstory** - Why this matters to you
4. **Solution intro** - What you discovered
5. **Key insight 1** - First lesson/feature
6. **Key insight 2** - Second lesson/feature
7. **Key insight 3** - Third lesson/feature
8. **Proof** - Results/testimonials
9. **CTA** - What to do next
10. **Engagement** - Ask a question

## Hashtag Strategy

### Twitter/X
- 1-2 hashtags maximum
- Place at end or integrate naturally
- Use niche hashtags over generic

### LinkedIn
- 3-5 hashtags maximum
- Mix of broad and niche
- Place at very end after signature line

## Engagement Tactics

### Thread Engagement
- Quote tweet your own thread with a hot take
- Reply to your own thread with bonus content
- Tag relevant people who might share

### Post Engagement
- Reply to every comment in first hour
- Ask a question at the end
- End with "Agree?" or "What would you add?"

### Algorithm Tips

**Twitter/X**
- Post when your audience is active
- Engage 15 mins before and after posting
- Use images/video for higher reach

**LinkedIn**
- Post early morning (7-8am) or lunch (12pm)
- No external links in main post (kills reach)
- First comment with link instead
