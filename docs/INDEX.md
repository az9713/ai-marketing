## What is AI Marketer?

AI Marketer is a **Claude Code plugin** that helps you write better marketing content for your software projects. It's based on 5 bestselling marketing psychology books and makes those frameworks accessible through simple commands.

### What It Does

- **Scores your headlines** against proven marketing frameworks
- **Audits your content** for persuasion elements
- **Generates optimized content** for GitHub, social media, and more
- **Analyzes competitors** to find differentiation opportunities
- **Extracts your voice** for consistent brand messaging

### Who It's For

- **Developers** who want their projects to get noticed
- **Open source maintainers** who need better READMEs
- **Startup founders** who can't afford marketing agencies
- **Anyone** who writes about technical products

## Prerequisites

Before using AI Marketer, you need:

1. **Claude Code** installed on your computer
2. **A Claude account** (free or paid)
3. **Basic familiarity with the terminal/command line**

Don't have Claude Code? See [Installing Claude Code](./user-guide/installing-claude-code.md).
